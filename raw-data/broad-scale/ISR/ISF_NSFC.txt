About the program
The program promotes research collaboration among Israeli and Chinese scientists, with support provided by an agreement between the Israel Science Foundation (ISF) and the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC)


Funding sources
Planning and Budgeting Committee (PBC) of the Council of Higher Education (CHE)
Dedicated budget provided by the Ministry of Finance to promote scientific collaboration with China

The team
The applications for this program are handled by the ISF team responsible for the International cooperation.


Submission rules
Eligibility: Open to researchers from all types of institutions according to the terms and guidelines for submission and the eligibility guide; this should be a joint submission together with a Chinese partner authorized to submit applications to the NSFC
Areas of research: Topics for this year are -
Exact Sciences and Technology: Chemistry, Computer Science, Earth Sciences, Mathematics, Nanotechnology, Physics

Amount of funding: Up to NIS 360,000 a year for three years (including overhead for eligible institutions); 
Number of grants per cycle: Up to 35

Review process
Reviews will be in done in parallel stages and coordinated between the ISF and the NSFC

Evaluations are conducted by professional committees in Israel and China

Evaluation criteria:

Scientific excellence
Proposal suitability
Capability to undertake the research
Benefits joint collaboration

Decision notification
Decisions will be communicated by the ISF to the submitting researcher(s), with relevant remarks from the review committee.

Grant activation
Grants are activated starting October 1st subject to approval and when signatures are received from researcher agreeing to the terms and conditions.
Annual reports detailing scientific progress and financial expenditure are required for the duration of the grant
A new grant will be activated only after all scientific obligations relating to any previous grant administered by the ISF have been concluded by the researcher
