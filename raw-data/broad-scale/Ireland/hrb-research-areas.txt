Basic research
    Basic research aims to improve the understanding of health or disease, with no direct application. 
Health services research
    Health services research aims to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of health professionals and the healthcare system. 
Patient-oriented and clinical research
    Patient-oriented and clinical research is conducted with human participants, or on material of human origin. 
Population health research
    Population health research has the goal of improving the health of the population, or of defined sub-populations.
