 Eurostars

Does your SME want to strengthen its competitiveness and develop an innovative product or service with cross border partner companies or research teams? The Eurostars programme will help you do this. Eurostars promotes cooperation with partners from 33 European countries as well as Canada, South Africa and South Korea in all thematic areas. The initiative is aimed in particular at innovative SMEs: SMEs that develop products or services that are significantly different from existing products or services.

You can submit your application for a Eurostars project through calls, usually twice a year.
This programme is aimed at:

    Innovative SMEs, large companies, and research institutions.
